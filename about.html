About

Hi, I'm Junior, and I have a passion for music! I enjoy listening to various genres, artists, and discovering new tunes. That's why I created the Paralax music search application that utilizes an iTunes API. With this app, you can easily search for your favorite music and artists, and it fetches information and displays it on the page. You can even play a snippet of the tracks and download them to your computer. It's all about enhancing your music experience! So, feel free to explore and enjoy the melodies. Feel free to go back to the Home Page to explore my other repositories.
